General Overseer & Founder
Bishop David
Adeyinka
"The church is not a building — it is the heartbeat of God pulsing through His people, alive in every act of love and service."

Bishop David Adeyinka founded Grace Chapel in 1994 with a congregation of twelve faithful members in a small rented hall in Lagos Island. Over three decades, under his anointed leadership, the church has grown into a vibrant family of thousands spread across multiple branches in Nigeria and beyond. A graduate of theology from the University of Lagos and the School of Biblical Studies, Bishop Adeyinka is a sought-after conference speaker, prolific author, and passionate advocate for community transformation. He is married to Pastor (Mrs.) Ruth Adeyinka, and they are blessed with four children.
