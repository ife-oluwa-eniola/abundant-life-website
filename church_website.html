About Us

More Than a Church — We're a Family

Founded on the foundation of love and Scripture, Abundant Life Assembly has been a beacon of hope in our community for over 30 years. We believe that every person is created with purpose and worthy of belonging.

  • Rooted in the Word of God and guided by the Holy Spirit
  • Committed to authentic worship and heartfelt prayer
  • Passionate about community outreach and servant leadership
  • A welcoming space for every generation and every background
