Meet Our General Overseer

A shepherd, a visionary, a servant of God

General Overseer & Founder
Pastor Ojo
Olukunmi
"The church is not a building — it is the heartbeat of God pulsing through His people, alive in every act of love and service."

Pastor Olukunmi Ojo founded Abundant Life Assembly, which was publicly known as The Bleeding Heart Mission in 1995 with a congregation of few members in a small rented hall in EPE. Over three decades, under his anointed leadership, the church has grown into a vibrant family across multiple branches in Lagos. A graduate of Fishery from Lagos State University, Pastor Olukunmi ojo is a sought-after conference speaker, prolific author, and passionate advocate for community transformation. He is married to Pastor (Mrs.) Taiwo Ojo, and they are blessed with Three children.
